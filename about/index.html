Extra

The Vocaholics

Music Director & Vice President (September 2015 - Present)

I am currently the Music Director and Vice President of the Vocaholics, NYU's premiere a cappella group. In this role, I lead and organize rehearsals to prepare the group for upcoming performances and assist in booking and managing performance opportunities and activities. I also write most of the musical arrangements we sing!

I've been a major part of leading the group to compete in the International Championship of Collegiate A Cappella (ICCA) for three years from 2017-2019. Most recently, the group won 1st Place at the Northeast Quarterfinal in Hofstra and I won the award for Outstanding Arrangement for the entire set. Last year, we won 3rd Place at the Northeast Quarterfinal.

In my time in the group, I've helped the group produce our album, "Everything's Fine", by managing production logistics, supervising the recording process, and writing eight of the ten song arrangements featured. It was a year long process from conception to finalization with a $10,000 production budget that we had to fundraise.
