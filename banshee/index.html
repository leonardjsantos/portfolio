LEONARD SANTOS

Banshee

The fantasy game where you get to play of the loudest creatures within the supernatural canon as you use your mystical abilities to evade demons and ward off their evil energies as you attempt to save the souls of recently departed human beings.

Controls

W ..... Jump
G ..... Glide
And every time you collect three souls, you gain the power to use the banshee scream! Activate this by screaming in real life.
