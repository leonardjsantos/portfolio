LGBTQ+ YOUTH MENTAL HEALTH IN THE MODERN AGE

by Leonard Santos

Digital Product: Website

Justification: The main purpose of my platform is to convey information as efficiently as possible that’s accessible to a wide net of people. This information also shouldn’t be placed within a linear narrative, since I want to help organizations, educators, and other advocates access the information they’re looking for as quickly as possible.

Audience: Organizations who want to provide support to LGBTQ+ youth through digital means

Requirements:

  • Fully coded website
  • Researched information and data
  • Interviews
  • Information from organizations and professionals
