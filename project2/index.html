Origin & Description

Our City Safe Haven is my research based project in which I built a website to explore mental health issues within the LGBTQ+ youth community. Building the website from scratch using HTML and CSS, I chose to look at the specific ecology in the West Village within New York City to look at the resources offered to young, queer people who might be suffering from depression, anxiety, suicidal thoughts, and other emotional and mental health concerns. The website also features photographs, audio tracks, and video tracks that were recorded and edited completely by me.

Process

Our City Safe Haven was actually my first practice-based digital media project, and the first time I ever learned HTML and CSS to build a website. I investigated a specific ecology within New York City; specifically, I researched the Village in Manhattan and the mental health of LGBTQ+ youth. Using my own equipment, I took all of the photos, audio tracks, and video tracks that are featured on the website and edited them all using Photoshop, Audition, and Premiere.
