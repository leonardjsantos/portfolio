Origin & Description

This video features me singing an a cappella arrangement of a mashup of Michael Jackson’s Thriller, Beyoncé Haunted, and Usher’s Scream with my a cappella group, the Vocaholics. As the Music Director and Vice President of the group, I arranged and music directed the song, while I had the help of Anthony Plantyn, who directed and edited this, Hannah Whitaker, who assistant directed, and the entire group to sing the piece.

Process

This began with an arrangement of the song that I wrote back in 2016, which the Vocaholics later used when competing in the International Championship of Collegiate A Cappella. We ended up putting the song into our third official album, Everything’s Fine. I helped supervise and direct everything that related to music, teaching the entire group the song and writing any new parts I wanted to add. Eventually, in the summer of 2018, the new executive board of the group voted to film a music video for the song.
