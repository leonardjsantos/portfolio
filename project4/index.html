Origin & Description

Escape from Apocalypse is a Processing-based game that surrounds a queer wizard as he tries to escape a dying relationship. The game features a movement mechanic as the player must navigate through the terrain to dodge enemies and obstacles, collect items and information, and eventually escape. The whole game is an allegory for the coming out experience, in which the realization of oneâ€™s sexuality can sometimes lead to a feeling of change.

Process

I based this game off of my own coming out experience, where I had felt that my surroundings no longer offered me a safe home to live in. Using characters that I designed and illustrated, I used Processing in order to make an abstract version of this experience reimagined in a magical space world.
