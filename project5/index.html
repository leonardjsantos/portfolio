Origin & Description

Rose-Tinted Theatre is a company dedicated to showcasing and reimagining existing theatrical narratives into joyful, triumphant stories about the LGBTQ+ community. As one of the founders and directors of the company, this is our official website that we are using to both promote the launch of Rose-Tinted as well as raise awareness and funds for our first production: a trans reimagining of Legally Blonde: The Musical. The website serves as a gateway for knowledge for the general public so that they may learn about our mission, donate to our most recent project, and join the team.

Process

I co-founded Rose-Tinted Theatre Company with my two best friends, Teagan Rabuano and Malichi Morris. Within this team, I specialize in media editing, web design, music directing and transposing, and other administrative tasks such as budgeting and scheduling. I coded the entire website using HTML and CSS, and I uploaded it online after buying a custom domain by going through GitHub Pages. The website, combined with our social media strategies using photographs and videos that I took and edited, have helped us gain $1,000 in donations, multiple applications to join the team, and hundreds of views, likes, and follows in Facebook and Instagram.
