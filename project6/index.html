Origin & Description

This project is a music visualization of the frequencies found within the tracks in the a cappella album, "Everything's Fine". The album was created by my a cappella group, the Vocaholics, where I've been the Music Director and Vice President for the past three years.

Process

As the Music Director and Vice President of my a cappella group, the Vocaholics, I was a key component in creating our third official studio album in 2018. I wrote eight of the ten of the arrangements that are featured in the album, and I helped organize the music direction, fundraising, and production of this piece.

Skills/Softwares/Languages

A Cappella Arranging, Sibelius (music writing software), JavaScript, p5, HTML, Music Directing
