FINAL PROJECT WRITEUP

LEONARD SANTOS

Under the Surface

Link to Game

Under the Surface is an escape the room adventure that tells the story of a young man who tries to bring his lover back to life. I used A-Frame virtual reality to create the entire project, and I wrote the story myself and designed most of the 2-D illustrations featured.

I started by making the environment and caged in the area using collision detection. Also, I used collision detection to create multiple floors so that the player could have more areas to explore. Afterwards, I created the key system so that the user had to press all of the keys in a certain order in order to finish the game, and also used distance calculation to figure out where the user was in relation to a key so that it could show a new diary entry. In order to make sure that the diary entry showed itself cleanly and that I could get out of it, I made invisible blocks to teleport to and HTML elements that I hid and revealed throughout the game.

Finally, I put down the aesthetic points of the game. I made a few revolving planets, created the sky, and also put in the assets for the stairs, floors, and walls for the environment. Also, I got 3-D models from Google Poly and also put in my own illustrations.

In the future, I'd like to create more levels and choices for the player and not condense the whole story into one whole floor.
